Tribute to the Indian Army

The Indian Army is a symbol of courage, sacrifice, and unwavering dedication to protecting our nation. Their commitment to defending our borders and ensuring our safety is unparalleled. They stand tall in the face of adversity and make incredible sacrifices for the security of our great nation.

The Indian Army's motto, "Service Before Self," reflects their selfless devotion to the country. They are not just soldiers; they are the guardians of our freedom and the protectors of our sovereignty.

"Our flag does not fly because the wind moves it, it flies with the last breath of each soldier who died protecting it." - Indian Army

Let us pay our deepest respects to the brave men and women of the Indian Army who put their lives on the line every day to keep our nation safe. Their sacrifices will forever be etched in the annals of our history.
